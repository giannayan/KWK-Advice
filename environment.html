Social Environment

Whether you're shy or nervous for the first day, we are here to prep you! This is a list of do's and don'ts.

    DO socialize with people during lunch and in between class, find out about where they're from, what grade they're going into, what are they're hobbies?
    DON'T curl up into a ball pretending to be looking at your phone but really just looking at a turned-off phone and staring at yourself and your lonely soul :)
    DO ask for help if you you need help. The teachers and TAs are here to help you, also, don't be afraid to ask your peers!
    DON'T skip out on the games, the games are so fun, my favorite personally is WHAH, or WAA idk. But definiteley participate in them with your full energy.
